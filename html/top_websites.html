top 10 most visited websites in the world

According to this year's report, based on Similarweb data collected between December 2021 and November 2022, Google remains the most visited website on the planet as of January 2023.

The Top 10 of the most popular pages on the Internet are made up of:

  1. Google.com
  2. YouTube.com
  3. Facebook.com
  4. Twitter.com
  5. Instagram.com
  6. Baidu.com
  7. Aliexpress.com
  8. Wikipedia.org
  9. Yahoo.com
  10. XVideos.com

When comparing the top with that of 2022, it can be seen that almost nothing has changed, in fact, the Top 5 has been maintained with the positions of Google, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. The only entry into the top 10 is Aliexpress, which ranked number 7.
In 2021, Yandex, the Russian Google, had entered the top 10, occupying the ninth place, but this year it did not occupy any position among the 20 most visited sites, something that is probably due to the Russia-Ukraine conflict. It can also be seen that the porn content pages remained within the top 20: Xvideos was ranked 10th and Pornhub ranked 12th.
Aliexpress enters the top 10 of the most visited websites in the world
The e-commerce of the Alibaba Group takes a spectacular leap in the ranking and is now in the seventh position of the websites with the most traffic in the world, although it is still very far from the undisputed leader, Google.com. Aliexpress is the first eCommerce in the table, with nearly twice as many visits as Amazon.

Social networks lead the top of the most visited websites in the world

The report shows that the average time Internet users spent browsing Google was 11 minutes and 09 seconds -21 seconds less than in 2021- with a total of 87.1 billion visits. For its part, YouTube managed to capture the attention of its users for an average of 21 minutes and 32 seconds, about 10 seconds less than the previous year.
In the case of Facebook, which registered 19.4 billion visits, with an average stay on the site of 9 minutes and 58 seconds (decreases for the second consecutive year).
Twitter, which in recent months has been visible to all, remained in fourth place in the top and registered 10 minutes with 45 seconds of permanence on average for users (only one second more than in 2021), in addition to registering at least 6.91 billion total views. Meanwhile, Instagram posted an average of 7 minutes 46 seconds of view time (5 seconds more than in 2021).
